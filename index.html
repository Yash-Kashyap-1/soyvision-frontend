Process

How It Works

Three steps from image to actionable insight.

01
Upload Image
Upload field image for weed detection, or soybean leaf image for disease detection. JPG, PNG, TIF supported.
02
Model Inference
Fine-tuned ResNet50 processes the image via Flask backend. Transfer learning from ImageNet weights.
03
Result + Grad-CAM
Get predicted class, confidence score, and Grad-CAM heatmap showing exactly which regions the model focused on.
